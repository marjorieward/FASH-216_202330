Class One - The Basic Five

✕

Before Class:

  • Complete the Pre-Quarter Assignment
  • Bring the following materials to class:
    • 1 yard of Oaktag Paper
    • 0.5 or 0.7 mm mechanical pencils
    • Erasers (if needed)
    • Paper Scissors
    • Utility knife or X-acto brand knife (optional)

During Class:

  • Trace and Cut the Basic Five Slopers in full scale and half scale.

After Class:

  • Cut out remainder of Full and Half Scale Slopers.
  • Include notches and be sure to label all slopers properly.
  • Complete oaktag slopers in preparation for Class 2.
